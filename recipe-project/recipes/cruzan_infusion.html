Cruzn Infusion

Description

This fruity drink is the perfect cocktail for beach parties, afternoon splashes at the pool, or Sunday brunch! Its origin is unknown, but is very popular on the Gulf Coast. It's colorful design and simplicity makes it a delicious, low-effort, tasty way to entertain your friends!

Mixing and matching ingredients is a fun way to change it up. Substitute any rum or mixer for another fruity option to enjoy the same great cocktail with a different twist!

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Cruzan Peach Rum
  • 1 oz Cruzan Coconut Rum
  • 1 oz Cruzan Strawberry Rum
  • Pineappble Juice
  • Lemonade
  • Orange Juice
  • Grenadine

Steps

  1. Fill a shaker with ice.
  2. Pour the Peach, Coconut, and Strawberry rums into a shaker, and fill with equal parts pineapple juice, orange juice, and lemonaded
  3. Pour the coctail into a glass.
  4. Add a splash of Grenadine to top it off.
