Black Cherry Whiskey Sour

Description

This Black Cherry Whiskey Sour Cocktail is here to simplify and elevate cocktail hours at your house. (Yes, hourS.) With a handful of smooth, simple ingredients, you'll be sipping this tempting concoction in mere minutes. Nothing frilly or stuffy about this pleasing mixed drink. Grab a glass, let's go!

Ingredients

  • Bulleit Bourbon
  • Lemon Juice
  • Agave Nectar
  • Black Cherry Juice
  • Seltzer Water
  • Cherry and Lemon Wedge

Steps

  1. In a short glass or Old Fashioned glass, pour in whiskey (or bourbon), lemon juice, agave, and cherry juice.
  2. Give a quick stir and add a large cocktail ice cube.
  3. Top with a little or a lot of seltzer water, depending on preference, and stir gently.
  4. Garnish with a cherry or lemon wedge.
